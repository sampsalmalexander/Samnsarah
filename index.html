After what can only be described as a very extended engagement, we have finally managed to find a gap in our calendars. Between work, travel, and generally pretending to be very busy people, we figured it was probably time to make it official.

What to wear

Black Tie · A Touch of Colour

Dress to impress — black tie is the standard, but we'd love to see a little colour woven in. Think soft peaches, apricots, lilacs and warm blush tones to complement the Yarra Valley at dusk.

For Her

  • Floor-length gown or elegant midi
  • Tailored jumpsuit or palazzo
  • Soft peach, apricot or lilac tones
  • Warm blush, champagne or sage
  • Avoid white, ivory & black only
  • Block heels recommended — lawn venue
