About Time

After what can only be described as a very extended engagement, we have finally managed to find a gap in our calendars. Between work, travel, and generally pretending to be very busy people, we figured it was probably time to make it official.

We got engaged doing what we love most — travelling — somewhere wonderful, slightly sunburnt, and very happy. The answer was yes before the question was even finished.

Now, we want to celebrate with the people who matter most to us. You made the list. We're so glad you're here.

What to wear

Black Tie · A Touch of Colour

Dress to impress — black tie is the standard, but we'd love to see a splash of colour. Think soft peaches, apricots, lilacs and warm blush tones to complement the Yarra Valley at dusk.

For Her

  • Floor-length gown or elegant midi
  • Tailored jumpsuit or palazzo
  • Peach, apricot or lilac tones
  • Blush, champagne or sage
  • Please avoid white & ivory
  • Block heels — it's a lawn venue

For Him

  • Black tuxedo or dark dinner suit
  • White dress shirt
  • Bow tie — add your colour here
  • Pocket square in peach or lilac
  • Navy or charcoal suit also welcome
  • Dress shoes — no sneakers

Zonzo Estate has gorgeous lawn areas — block heels or elegant flats are your best friend.
